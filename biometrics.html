Bometrics & Access Control

The security of your employees, as well as your company’s equipment and data, is critical. Traditional key cards and pass codes are excellent tools to have in place, but access can be transferred. Key cards can be stolen,and pass codes can be given or discovered by prying eyes. Even with excellent security options in place, your security can be compromised.
Biometric scanners are advanced access control options.
You use body parts, such as fingerprints or your face, to grant access to a secure environment. Biometric scanners are frequently used in high security areas of facilities such as labs, information storage areas . One of the most popular biometric scanners is a fingerprint scanner. These are becoming widely popular in Access Control Management systems. Fingerprint scanners work by determining if the image of your fingertip matches the ridges and valleys in a previously scanned image. Because no two fingertips are exactly alike, no individual can access an area using their fingertip and claim it as another’s.

Temperature /Facial Recognition Access Control Reader

DTEC Systems is introducing the newly released facial recognition with body temperature
detection system which have become a real ‘need to have’ especially in recent times-Covid 19
Pandemic.
It helps ALERT and RESTRICT people having elevated body temperatures from
entering large highly dense public places including industrial premises, office spaces and
many other public buildings.
The access control readers’ facial recognition and temperature measurement
capabilities are able to identify individuals with abnormally high temperatures during
biometric verification process.
Audio and visual signals are used to indicate an attempt to gain access by an individual
with an abnormally high temperature [Temperature outside the accepted values]
Advantages of the Temperature/Facial Recognition Reader:
✓ Automatic-no need to employ somebody to scan people entering some premises
✓ Avoids risking exposure to those scanning employees or visitors[unmanned]
✓ Can be integrated with existing access control systems.
